Authentic Mexican Cuisine / Auténtica Comida Mexicana

La Corona Bar and Grill brings the authentic flavors of Jalisco to Los Angeles. Established in 2018, we are known for our delicious molcajetes, enchiladas, and traditional Mexican dishes made with rich flavor and care. From the moment you walk in, our décor, flooring, and furniture create a warm atmosphere that makes you feel like you are in Mexico.

Owner Humberto R. has been part of the family restaurant business since 1992, when he opened his first business with his father, Salvador, in Reseda, California. With more than 26 years of experience, Humberto now brings his passion and knowledge to La Corona Bar and Grill, working alongside his brothers to continue sharing authentic Mexican food with the community.
